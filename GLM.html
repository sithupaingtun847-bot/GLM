About Us

GLM Computer Class helps students learn modern digital skills including coding, design, and real-world tech applications. Our mission is to prepare you for the future.
I started my computer class in 2022 with a simple goal—to share my passion for technology. What began as helping a few students quickly grew into a structured class. Today, it’s a place where learners build skills, confidence, and creativity in a supportive and inspiring environment.

Web Development
